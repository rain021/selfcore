# SelfCore — ProductHunt Submission

## Tagline (60 chars max)
Own your AI identity. One file. Every AI.

## Description (260 chars max)
SelfCore creates a portable .self profile with your identity, skills, and preferences. Inject it into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok — every AI knows you instantly. 100% local. Your data never leaves your PC.

## Maker Comment

Hey ProductHunt! 👋

I built SelfCore because I was tired of re-explaining myself every time I switched between AI tools.

**The problem:** Every AI forgets you. Switch from ChatGPT to Claude? Start over. Open Gemini? Explain everything again. Your context is trapped inside each platform.

**The solution:** SelfCore creates a single .self file — your portable AI identity. Press Ctrl+Shift+Space, and any AI instantly knows your name, skills, projects, and preferences.

**What makes it different:**
🧠 Imports your ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok history to build your profile automatically
🔒 100% local — your data never leaves your PC. Not even we can see it.
📦 One .self file works with every AI platform
⚡ Smart context routing — only sends relevant info (under 200 tokens)
🌍 English + Korean UI

**Tech:** Electron + Python + spaCy NLP + optional local LLM (Ollama)
**License:** GPL-3.0 (open source)

I'd love to hear your feedback. What features would make this more useful for your AI workflow?
